## Incident response for critical infrastructure

This playbook focuses on a triage workflow for ransomware, phishing, and enterprise compromise.

### Priorities

1. Isolate affected segments.
2. Collect forensic evidence.
3. Validate backups and recovery options.
4. Coordinate with law enforcement and CERT-In.

### Sample email triage checklist

- Identify sender and reply path.
- Preserve original headers.
- Analyze attachments in a sandbox.
- Notify support teams of confirmed phishing.

### Executive summary

Modern incident response must balance containment with operational continuity for regional enterprises.
