## Odisha SOC design principles

The Odisha Cyber Operations platform brings environment-specific threat intelligence, advisory aggregation, and detection engineering into a unified hub.

### Key mission areas

- Live threat tracking for regional campaigns
- Advisory aggregation from CISA, Microsoft, Unit 42, and CERT-In
- Incident response workflows for fraud, ransomware, and supply chain compromise

### Detection engineering sample

```yaml
rule: odisha_ransomware_command_shell
logsource:
  category: process_creation
  product: windows
condition:
  selection:
    CommandLine|contains:
      - "powershell"
      - "Invoke-Expression"
  condition: selection
```

### Takeaway

A strong regional hub must combine local awareness with global threat feeds to reduce blast radius and improve response time.
